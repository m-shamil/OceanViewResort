Ocean View

Hotel Management · Official Receipt
Thank you for choosing Ocean View Hotel
Login
Enter your username and password on the login screen. Contact your system administrator if you have forgotten your credentials.
Staff AccountsAdmin Only
  • Create — Fill in all fields and click "Create Account" to register a new staff member.
  • Edit — Click the edit button on a staff row to update details.
  • Delete — Click the trash icon and confirm the deletion prompt.
  • View — All active and inactive staff appear in the directory table.
Reservations
  • New Reservation — Fill in guest details, select room type, and set check-in / check-out dates. The system will automatically check for room conflicts.
  • Update Status — Use the dropdown in the Actions column to update reservation status.
  • Cancel — Click the cancel icon next to a reservation to cancel it.
Billing
Calculate Bill
Enter Reservation Number to compute total with 12% tax applied
Print Bill
Click "Print Bill" to send the receipt directly to your printer
Guest E-Bill Access
Guests can view their e-bill on the login screen using only their reservation number — no account needed
Exit / Logout
Click the Exit / Logout button at the bottom of the sidebar to securely end your session and return to the login screen.
